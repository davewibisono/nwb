### Governance Section
governance_section:
    enable: true 
    title: "Governance Structure"
    content: "To guide the next phase of growth, and to coordinate the various efforts, NWB has created a governance structure. This will allow NWB to grow in a bottom-up, but coordinated manner. Participation in the project is open to any interested neuroscientist, with overall planning coordinated by an Executive Board (EB) and overall technical development coordinated by a Technical Advisory Board (TAB)."

### Executive Section
executive_section:
    enable: true
    title: "The Executive Board"
    content: "The executive board consists of seven members, each appointed for a term of 2-3 years. The EB will perform the following duties:"
    list:
        - "Develop NWB policies, including issue regarding licensing, code repositories, etc"
        - "Define the scope of NWB"
        - "Organize sustainable funding for NWB"
        - "Coordinate a unified and coherent fund-raising strategy"
        - "Coordinate communication regarding NWB, including web sites, community outreach, publications"
        - "Make strategic decisions"
        - "Organize and oversee the activities of the core development team"
        - "Provide input for major technical decisions to the core development team"
